Game Description

  • Welcome to Heroes of Match 3, an exciting match-3 adventure set in a medieval world full of sweets and challenges!
  • Dive into a magical kingdom, match delicious treats, and help King Donuttan The Third reclaim his throne.
  • Each level presents strategic puzzles, where you must combine sweets to defeat enemies and progress through the royal saga.
  • With engaging characters, special power-ups, and challenging levels, this is not just another match-3 game—it’s an epic quest!
  • Who knows, maybe YOU will become the next ruler of the kingdom?
